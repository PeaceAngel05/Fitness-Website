Introduction

Fitness is assumed by many to be achieved by only going to the gym.As much as exercise is a key element in achieving fitness,other things like good sleeping habits and a healthy diet are major factors to consider.Staying hydrated is also important when it comes to health and fitness.

Calories

Calories are important when it comes to fitness since they give our bodies the energy we need to perform day to day functions.Calories have a direct effect on our weight .If we want to loss weight we must consume less calories than we burn .If we want to retain weight the number of calories we burn must be propotional to the number of calories we consume.when it comes to exercise and physical activity the more exercise you do the higher the number of calories your body burns .so for you to achieve a desired result the number of calories you consume will be a major factor
